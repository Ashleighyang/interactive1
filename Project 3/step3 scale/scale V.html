How Much A "Big V" User Weight?


Followers

Weight is the priority that website values users. "Big V" users usually refers to the users who own over 500,000 followers. The website will display their status, moment, answers on the timeline of all the users, and recommend them to new users to follow at the beginning they browse. However, the number of Big V users is lower than 1% of total users, which means based on the design of website, 1% of people decide what the rest of users can see.
